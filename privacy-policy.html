Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

✓ This app collects no personal data

1. Overview

Tamil Panchangam ("the App") is a daily Vedic almanac app for Tamil-speaking communities worldwide. We are fully committed to your privacy. This policy explains our data practices clearly and simply.

2. Data We Collect

We collect absolutely no personal data. There is no account, no login, no registration, and no tracking of any kind.

  • No name, email, or phone number
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting
  • No third-party SDKs for tracking or advertising

3. Location Data

The App may request access to your device's location solely to calculate accurate local sunrise, sunset, Rahu Kalam, and Muhurtham times for your city. This is done entirely on your device. Your coordinates are never transmitted to any server, stored, or shared with anyone.

You can deny location permission at any time — the app will fall back to a default city. You can also manually select any city from the built-in list.

4. Internet Access

On first launch, the App loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This is a standard web request with no personal data. After the first load, fonts are cached and no further internet access is required. The App functions fully offline after that.

5. Data Storage

Your selected city preference is saved locally on your device only, using your device's standard storage. This data never leaves your device.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any advertising networks, analytics platforms, social media SDKs, or any other third-party services that collect data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and collects no data from any user regardless of age. It is safe and appropriate for all ages including children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: support@tamilvaram.com
