What's inside

Everything you need, every day

Five Panchangam elements, accurate local timings, and a full Tamil calendar — all in one clean app.

🌙
Five Panchangam Angas
Tithi, Vaaram, Nakshatram, Yogam and Karanam calculated fresh every day using precise lunar and solar algorithms.
⚠️
Rahu Kalam & Inauspicious Times
Rahu Kalam, Gulikai and Yamagandam calculated from your actual local sunrise — not a fixed Chennai time applied everywhere.
✨
Auspicious Muhurthams
Abhijit, Brahma Muhurtham, Amruta Kalam and more — with plain English descriptions of what each is good for.
☀️
Live Sunrise Tracker
Visual arc showing exactly where the sun is right now between your local sunrise and sunset.
🗓
Tamil Calendar
Full monthly calendar with Tamil date numbers on every day. Tap any date to see its Tithi, Nakshatram and Yogam.
📍
Works Worldwide
GPS auto-detection or choose from 23 cities across India, Southeast Asia, Europe, Americas, Africa and Oceania.
